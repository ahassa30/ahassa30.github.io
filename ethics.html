IT Ethics

Ethical considerations in the IT field are important for maintaining trust with everyone. Technology provides many benefits but can also lead to harmful outcomes. Here are some ethical issues affecting IT in today's society:

  • Misinformation and Deepfake: Privacy invasion and misuse of identity result in personal information being exploited inappropriately.
  • Lack of General Oversight: Companies hiring third-party tech for critical data oversight can lead to confusion about responsibility.
  • Significant Use of Artificial Intelligence: Misuse of facial recognition software can result in job displacement or racial bias.
